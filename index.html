Driver CPC & Walkaround Check

Learn about the Certificate of Professional Competence requirements for bus drivers and the critical pre-journey vehicle inspection.

Why Walkaround Checks Are Essential

The Driver CPC (Certificate of Professional Competence) is mandatory for all professional bus and coach drivers in Ireland. It ensures that drivers have the necessary skills and knowledge to operate vehicles safely.

A thorough walkaround check is a critical part of the CPC test and daily professional practice. It helps identify potential vehicle defects before starting a journey, ensuring passenger safety and compliance with regulations.

Our training materials cover all aspects of the walkaround check, from inspecting tires and lights to checking fluid levels and emergency equipment.

Based on the official CPC syllabus, our resources help you prepare for both the theory and practical elements of the test.
